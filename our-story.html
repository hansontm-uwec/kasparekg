Our Story

It started with a love of authentic food. In 2021, we decided we wanted to bring a space to this city that served real American/French inspired food. We started with a breakfast menu and thought, "why stop there?" so we continued expanding our menu to include brunch and lunch foods, as well. Not only do we want what we serve to be authentic, we also want it to be fresh. All of our food is made with local and fresh ingredients, straight from the source to your plate.
